Have a Coffee
Have a Coffee is a warm and inviting coffee business that aims to elevate your coffee experience. We specialize in carefully sourced beans, expertly roasted to bring out the unique flavors of each origin. From rich and bold espressos to smooth and creamy lattes, we offer a wide range of handcrafted beverages to satisfy every coffee lover's palate. Our cozy atmosphere and friendly baristas ensure that every visit is a delightful moment to sit back, relax, and have a coffee.
Different Types Of Coffee We Serve
Brazilian Coffee
Known for its smooth, nutty flavor with low acidity, making it versatile for various brewing methods.
Ethiopian Coffee
Renowned for its fruity and floral notes, Ethiopian beans can offer a complex and vibrant flavor profile.
Colombian Coffee
Typically medium-bodied with a balanced acidity, Colombian beans often have notes of caramel and citrus.
Guatemalan Coffee
Offers a rich and full-bodied profile with hints of chocolate, spice, and sometimes fruity undertones.
